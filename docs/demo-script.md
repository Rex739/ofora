# Ofora Demo Script

## 0:00-0:15 Problem

Procurement teams are forced to choose between supplier confidentiality and public trust.

## 0:15-0:35 Ofora

Ofora locks the selection rules before suppliers submit, then independently validates the final award.

## 0:35-1:05 Rules And Submissions

Show the Emergency Solar Lantern Procurement tender. The requirements and priorities are locked first. Then Atlas, Nova, and Meridian submit confidential proposals. The demo does not expose their commercial details.

## 1:05-1:25 Meridian

Open Meridian. Meridian is not eligible because delivery exceeds the 14-day requirement.

## 1:25-1:45 Atlas

Select Atlas. Atlas is eligible, but the award is blocked because another eligible supplier achieved a higher score under the locked rules.

## 1:45-2:10 Nova

Select Nova. The award validates and Ofora shows Fair Award Receipt `FAR-OFR-2026-041-NOVA`.

## 2:10-2:35 Stellar

Show the Stellar testnet evidence: the verification receipt transaction, the registry finalization transaction, the verifier receipt contract, and the registry contract. Point out that competing commercial information remains protected.

## 2:35-2:50 Close

Ofora makes confidential bids defensible.
